To all units in the
northern sector. Allied forces have
been detected advancing through the
Ardennes forest region. Intelligence reports
indicate enemy convoy movement along
route seven four scheduled for
zero six hundred hours tomorrow.
All divisions are ordered to
maintain defensive positions and await
further instructions. Supply shipments including
ammunition and fuel reserves will
arrive at designated coordinates by
nightfall. Weather conditions expected to
deteriorate with heavy fog predicted
for early morning hours which
may provide tactical advantage. Radio
silence must be maintained except
for emergency communications. Reconnaissance patrols
should report any unusual enemy
activity immediately to headquarters. All
personnel are reminded that vigilance
and discipline remain essential to
mission success.
